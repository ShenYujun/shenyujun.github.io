Yujun Shen (Damon)



                   

Biography

Yujun Shen is currently a senior research scientist at Ant Research, leading the Interatcion Intelligence Lab. His research focuses on computer vision, deep learning, and representation learning, particularly on 2D&3D generative models and the downstream applications.
